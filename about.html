About Me

I grew up in California and served a two-year mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Southern Idaho. I'm currently studying at BYU-Idaho, pursuing a major in Mathematics with a minor in Statistics. My academic interests span both the technical and humanistic—I'm drawn to the rigor of mathematics and low-level programming while equally passionate about philosophy, theology, history, civics, and classical literature. I know English and Greek, and have served as a teaching assistant for humanities courses, which has deepened my appreciation for how technical and humanistic thinking can inform each other.
