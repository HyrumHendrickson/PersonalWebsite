Research Interests

Areas of active investigation and exploration

Computational Philosophy

Exploring how computational methods can illuminate philosophical problems and vice versa, bridging abstract reasoning with algorithmic implementation.

Mathematical Foundations of AI

Investigating the mathematical principles underlying machine learning and artificial intelligence systems to improve theoretical understanding.

Epistemology of Data Science

Examining how we gain knowledge from data and the philosophical implications of statistical inference and machine learning models.

Ethics of Technology

Studying the moral implications of technological advancement and AI development, particularly in areas of algorithmic fairness and responsibility.

Logic & Reasoning Systems

Developing formal systems for automated reasoning and logical inference, with applications in artificial intelligence and knowledge representation.

Historical Analysis

Applying quantitative methods to historical research and understanding historical patterns through computational and statistical approaches.
